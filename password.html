About This Password Checker

Be cautious! Entering credentials into untrusted websites can lead to hacking.

This Password Strength Meter is safe because:

  • Your passwords never leave your browser.
  • All checking is done on the page, not on servers.
  • Even if sent, we cannot match it to any usernames or websites.
Strong Password Tips and Tricks

Common mistakes:

  • Replacing letters with digits/symbols doesn't improve security.
  • Meeting minimum password length isn't sufficient.
  • Using the same password across sites can lead to multiple breaches.

Make passwords at least 14 characters long. Consider using passphrases for better security!
